1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register as a parent, we collect: name, email address, contact number, and basic information about your children (name, age, school class).

Assessment Data

We collect dyslexia and dysgraphia screening results, including speech recordings, handwriting images, and generated scores. This data is used solely for educational intervention purposes.

Technical Information

We collect device information, browser type, IP address, and usage statistics to improve platform functionality and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

Personalized Intervention

To generate customized 4-week curriculum plans based on assessment results.

Progress Tracking

To monitor child progress and adjust intervention strategies.

Communication

To send important updates about assessments and curriculum progress.

Platform Improvement

To enhance our detection algorithms and user experience.

3. Data Security & Protection

Encryption

All data is encrypted both in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

Access Control

Strict role-based access ensures only authorized personnel can view sensitive data.

Secure Storage

Data is stored on secure servers with regular security audits and monitoring.

4. Your Rights

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You can update or correct inaccurate information at any time.

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Questions About Privacy?

Our Data Protection Officer is available to answer any questions about our privacy practices.

Contact Data Protection Officer

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
